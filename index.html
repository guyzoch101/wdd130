Hong Kong

Hong Kong is a special administrative region in China, a city in China. Located at the southern tip of China, the weather here is very hot and humid, with typhoons approaching during summer. Besides of the weather, it is a paradise of shopping and food. There's no other place like Hong Kong, a city that never sleeps, yet Hong Kongers are always proud to call this tiny city home.
